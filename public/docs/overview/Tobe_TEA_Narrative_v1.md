# The Opportunity

## The Problem No One's Solved

Grey hydrogen costs $2/kg to produce.

But you pay $15–25/kg delivered.

**85% of what you're paying isn't hydrogen. It's logistics.**

Grey hydrogen only makes economic sense when manufactured at enormous scale in centralized hubs — Gulf Coast, Alberta, the same facilities that have dominated the industry for 50 years. Every industrial customer more than 100 miles from a hub is captive to trucking economics.

94 million tonnes per year. $226 billion in annual demand. All locked into a model where the product is cheap but moving it is ruinous.

---

## What We Built

Tobe Energy produces hydrogen on-site using membrane-free electrolysis with pulsed waveform architecture.

- **No precious metals.** No membranes. No rare earths.
- **Near-ambient operation.** No chillers, no thermal management.
- **>92% system efficiency (HHV).** Up to 94.7% at the stack.
- **First electrolyzer under DOE's $2/kg target** at industrial electricity rates.

We don't ship hydrogen. We ship machines that make it.

## Key Risks & Mitigations

### Technology Risk
**Status:** Proven at prototype scale. T-25 in production. T-125 at Zeeco ARC commissioning Q2 2026.

**Mitigation:** Third-party efficiency validation scheduled. UL 2264A certification in progress (targeting Q4 2026 — Ohmium was first certified Nov 2024, demonstrating clear pathway).

### Operational Risk
**Status:** First commercial deployment. Limited operating history at scale.

**Mitigation:** Zeeco partnership provides ARC facility infrastructure, operational support, and reference customer. EPC capability de-risks deployment execution.

### Maintenance & Durability
**Status:** Long-duration testing ongoing. Modeled at 5% annual replacement reserve.

**Mitigation:** Membrane-free design eliminates primary degradation mechanism of PEM systems. No precious metal catalyst degradation.

### Market Risk
**Status:** $100M pipeline. First deployment contracted.

**Mitigation:** Pricing at $25/kg positions significantly below grey hydrogen delivered cost ($15–25) while maintaining 70%+ gross margins. Room to compete on price if needed.

### Regulatory Risk
**Status:** 45V guidance clarifying. BABA compliance achieved.

**Mitigation:** 95% US-sourced content. Oklahoma manufacturing provides political and supply chain resilience.

## Why This Works

**1. Grey hydrogen's business model is broken.**
85% of delivered cost is logistics. The entire industry is built on moving a commodity that's cheap to make but expensive to transport.

**2. We eliminated the cost stack's biggest line item.**
On-site production. No trucking. No storage terminals. No hazmat logistics.

**3. The margins are structural.**
At $2.62/kg production cost and $25/kg delivered pricing, we're not competing on efficiency improvements. We're competing on business model.

**4. The market isn't going anywhere.**
94M tonnes/year of required industrial demand. Refining, ammonia, steel. These aren't discretionary. They're locked in.

**5. The timing is now.**
45V provides $3/kg tailwind for the next decade. Facilities built before 2028 capture $27M+ per site in tax credits.
