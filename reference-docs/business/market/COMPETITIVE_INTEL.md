## 💡 Key Talking Points for Investors

1. **"Everyone else uses membranes. We don't."** - Nel, Plug, ITM all depend on expensive membranes that degrade. Tobe's membrane-free architecture eliminates this cost and failure mode.

2. **"No rare earth metals."** - PEM electrolyzers need iridium ($50K+/kg) and platinum. Tobe uses commodity stainless steel. Supply chain risk = zero.

3. **"Cold operation."** - SOEC runs at 800°C. PEM at 50-80°C. Alkaline at 70-90°C. Tobe runs at <30°C. No active cooling = smaller footprint, lower BOP cost.

4. **"94% vs 65-75%."** - That's not incremental improvement. That's a generational leap. Like going from incandescent to LED.

5. **"Made in America."** - 95% US-sourced. In a world of reshoring and IRA domestic content requirements, this matters.

6. **"The market is exploding."** - $1.8B → $281B by 2033. Even a tiny slice of that is a massive company.
